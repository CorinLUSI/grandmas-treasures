Our Policies

Shipping & Cancellation

Shipping Policy

Please be advised that only jams can be shipped. They will be shipped for arrival within two (2) business days max. via Fedex. Cakes must be picked up on location within the time specified by phone or email confirmation.

You can order our products up to three months in advance. All you have to do is indicate which date you want your products to arrive and we will deliver them to you as requested.

Due to high demand, orders must be placed at least one (1) week in advance. If you need your product(s) to be ready sooner, please notify us and we will be more than happy to try and accommodate you. Note, however, that an additional fee may apply for orders requiring immediate attention.

Cancellation Policy

Products must be paid for in full at the time of purchase. Please keep in mind that all of our products are homemade and artisanal in nature and we therefore produce our products on a reservation schedule. In the event that you choose to cancel your purchase, the following schedule of cancellation charges shall apply to the original purchase amount:

  • Up to and including thirty (30) days prior to shipment date: 0% of the purchase amount
  • Less than thirty (30) days prior to the shipment date: 10% of the purchase amount
  • Less than fifteen (15) days prior to the shipment date: 25% of the purchase amount
  • Less than seven (7) days prior to the shipment date: 50% of the purchase amount
