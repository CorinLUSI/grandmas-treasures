Handmade Jams

Our gourmet homemade jams are from grandma's old fashioned recipes, featuring high quality fruit and zero artificial ingredients. Our jams contain no artificial colors or additives and are nut- and gluten-free. They all have a very natural, fresh taste based on three parts fruit and only one part sugar. They are produced using seasonal fruits, which make them even more flavorful and delicious!
